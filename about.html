Sayd Haque is a final year student at the Department of Mass Communication with specialized focus on electronic media at International Islamic University Malaysia (IIUM). Currently, he is working at OIC International Business Centre as Head of Media & Creative. Moreover, he is serving as CEO & Co-Founder of Illusion Pictures, a production house established with some visions to work for the betterment of the society, and at the same time, to bring new and innovative ideas in film making and advertising. Sayd has done some good quality works that include Advertisements, Short films, Talk Shows, Event Promos, Documentaries, Short films, Graphic Design and so on.

As a freelance producer, editor, director, director of photography (DOP) and Graphic Designer, he has also worked for companies and institutions such as MayBank Islam, NV1, TV Alhijrah, NTV, OIC TODAY Magazine, Abduhu Films, IIUM, Mercy Mission, Twins of Faith, Muslim World Biz, IIUM.fm, Being Me (Women Conference), Marriage Concurrence, Ilm Art Festival, Australia base My Prayer Project, Araaq, Elicos Malaysia, Achlim, and so on.
